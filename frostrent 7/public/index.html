Why it's safe

Ownership never actually changes hands

Rentals run on Steam's own trade-lock system, not trust.

Trade-lock protected

Every rented item is locked to the renter's account for the rental duration — it can't be resold or traded away while it's rented. When time's up, a return request is sent automatically — accept it, done.

Paid before it moves

Skins are only sent once the rental payment clears — owners never send an item on trust, and renters never pay for a skin that doesn't arrive.

Read-only account access

We only ever need your trade URL to scan and move items — never your Steam password or Steam Guard codes.
